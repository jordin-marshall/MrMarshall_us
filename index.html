About Your Teacher

Graduating from Jeffersonville High School in 1993, Mr. Marshall began coaching baseball at Jeff the followin year. Not only has he coached baseball, but he has coached basketball at Charlestown middle and Jeff High. While some may still consider him young in age, Mr. Marshall, post-graduation, has been apart of the Jeff High Program for the last 20 years. Spending his first two years as a teacher at Parkview and Charlestown, Mr. Marshall has since resided at his alma mater. Mr. Marshall earned his education degree while attending I.U.S. His wife, and two oldest children have both attended and graduated Jeffersonville and his oldest is currently a student at Bellarmine University. His two youngest children are both beginning elementary school.
